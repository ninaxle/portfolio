Hi, I am Nina (she/her), a fourth year design student at York University. I was always on the creative part of the internet. I spent my early childhood watching digital speedpaints, which taught me how to draw. The best speedpaints were always the ones with nightcore or nu metal music, which was (probably) what got me into singing. I eventually picked up on how to code.

As a ux/ui designer, I now bring a multidisciplinary approach that combines aesthetic sensibility with functional precision. My background in both design and development allows me to create experiences that are not only visually compelling but technically sound.
