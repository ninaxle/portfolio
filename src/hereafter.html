Here:after – A Journaling App for Students

Overview

Here:after is a journaling application designed to simplify self-reflection for students. This project emerged from an assignment for my interaction design class, but evolved into a solution that accomplished a first place solution in the UX category of 2023 RGD student awards :)

AWARD

RDG UX Tie-Winner, 2023

TOOLS

Zoom, FigJam, Figma

TIMELINE

January 13, 2023 – April 7, 2023

TEAM

Jersey Stuart, Rita Zhan, Shriya Mujumdar

Problem Statement

How might we create a simple and convenient journaling app to help students consistently reflect and improve their emotional well-being?
