Radiate Showcase Website

Radiate is York University's DESN 2026 Grad Show brand, celebrating the 44 design graduates behind the screen. I led a team of 7 designers to develop the front facing website: product and design for the site end-to-end: running the team's process, and acting as the communication layer between branding, external relations, and the web build.

The Problem Statement

Grad-show sites fade out students into a grid of portfolios, making it hard for employers to judge fit, and easy for strong candidates to get lost among similar-looking work.

Style Guide

Our group developed the style guide with a focus on creating an uplifting and positive experience for users. In this process, I carefully selected soothing colors, such as soft pastels and cool tone, to evoke feelings of calm and reassurance, while ensuring they met WCAG color contrast guidelines for accessibility.

Final Outcomes

Upon shipping, The site has reached roughly 3,000 users per month, serving as the primary touchpoint between the Class of 2026 and the employers, peers, and industry professionals discovering their work. After the event was over, we updated the gallery to represent people’s booths at the exhibition.
